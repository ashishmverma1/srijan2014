FAQs

Q1. What is the registration fee?
The registration fee is Rs.1000 for on-spot registration and Rs.850 for online payment before 25th March. Also, for registration, fill in the form available in the registration section beforehand. Also, Rs.100 extra will be taken as caution money from the participant on the registration desk and will be refunded when the participant chooses to leave, after the due checking of the services i.e. bedding accommodation provided to them by ISM Dhanbad.

Q2. How do I pay the registration fee?
 The registration fee would be collected once you reach the registration desk of SRIJAN’14. And if you choose to pay by online mode, you’ll be required to transfer the fees to SRIJAN account, details of which are mentioned below. Don’t forget to write Srijan Registration in the remarks section while paying the fees. Afterwards, fill in the registration form on the website and mention your transaction ID.

Q3. In which account do I deposit the online registration fees?
Account No.: 10230795114
Bank Name: SBI (ISM Branch, Dhanbad)
IFS Code: SBIN0001641
MICR #: 826002006

Q4. What all deliverables do we receive after registering?
 Registration fee includes exclusive SRIJAN’14 T-shirts, accommodation, goodies and an id-card which ensures the participant gets free entry into all-star nights . Winners get internship opportunities (selected events) apart from the cash awards given and are also certified for their participation as well as for winning the events.

Q5. What kind of accommodation is available?
 There is separate accommodation for girls and boys. Proper bedding and blankets would be made available to all the registered students.

Q6. Is food included in the registration fee?
 No, food is not included in the registration amount. However, there are food stalls available in the arena open till night. Apart from this, there are various canteens in the campus where food is available at affordable rates.

Q7. Can I register, but not take accommodation?
 The registration amount remains unchanged in both the cases. It is advisable to avail the accommodation facility since there is no other mode of accommodation available in the campus during the fest.

Q8. How do I avail travelling facility from Dhanbad Station to ISM?
 To avail service from Dhanbad station to ISM, volunteers will be available at Dhanbad station.

Q9. I want to attend star nights only, will I have to pay the whole registration fee?
 In such a case, you would have to pay a sum of Rs 300 for the pro night of 30th March at the registration desk to get special pass for star night or you can also opt to pay online Rs 250 beforehand. Don’t forget to write Srijan Star Night in the remarks section while paying the fees. Afterwards, fill in the registration form on the website and mention your transaction ID. But in this case, you cannot avail accommodation, nor can you participate in any other event.

Q10. How do we register for a group event?
For group registration, all team members are required to register themselves individually to get their SRIJAN IDs first. Then one member of the team will send a mail with the subject as "event name" for that particular event to the ‘email-id’ specified in the rules of that particular event. The mail should comprise the following details:

  • College Name
  • Names of all the team members
  • SRIJAN ID’s of all team members
  • Phone numbers (of atleast two members)
  • E-mail ID's (of atleast two members)
  • Location (City)
Please note that pre-registration for all events is mandatory. The organizers reserve the right to disqualify any participating team which has not pre-registered.

Q11. Can two participants be from different colleges in a team event?
Yes. SRIJAN does not restrict cross participation from colleges in a team event.

Q12. I am coming to Srijan with lots of friends from my college. Do I get a bulk discount?
Yes. If your group comprises of more than 30 students, you can apply for concession and Rs 150 per head would be refunded in case of on-site payment of registration fees and Rs 100 per head would be refunded in case of online payment of registration fees. However, be advised that this is applicable on students from the same college only.

Q13. What are Srijan IDs?
Srijan ID’s are unique identification codes issued to participants as they register themselves. Participants are required to report with their ISrijan ID’s at the control tent and that will be required for further participation in any event.

Q14. How to pay the online fees?
An account transfer should be made to the account number mentioned on the Registration page. Your transaction ID will be verified on the registration desk. Any failure to bring the transaction slip, would void the registration.

About Srijan

Srijan. Call it the 'passion for creation' or a 'celebration of creativity'; its all about setting our wildest dreams and passions into motion. Srijan is celebrated annually at ISM Dhanbad. The festival is not only a panorama of events to test the cultural and artistic skills of the youth from all over the country, but it also endeavours to bring together eminent artists as well as celebrities across the globe, all in an attempt to bestow a memorable and spellbinding experience.

About Indian School of Mines

The Indian School of Mines was formally opened on 9th December 1926, by Lord Irwin, the then Viceroy of India with disciplines of Mining and Applied Geology. In 1967 it was granted the status of a deemed to be university. Situated in the heart of the country's prime coking coal belt, the campus spreads over an area of 393 acres. The fully residential campus with all the facilities of a world class academic institute currently has 17 different Departments and over 200 faculty, and over 5000 students.

The institute currently offers a host of programmes like B. Tech., M. Tech., M. Sc. Tech., and MBA. In addition the School also offers M. Phil. and full time as well as part time Ph. D. programmes in the field of engineering, Sciences, Management and Humanities and Social Sciences. ISM also awards D.Sc. as the highest degree of academic achievement. The admission to the four year and the five year graduate courses are through the prestigious IIT-JEE examination. The institute has links with reputed universities and institutes across the globe and has an alumni base all over the world. Today the School is making foray into the newer areas of academic endeavors in tune with the changing ties and demands of the industries.
